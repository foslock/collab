How to use Collab Canvas

Draw
Click and drag on the canvas to draw. Release to finish a stroke.
Pan
Hold Shift and drag, or drag with the middle mouse button.
Zoom
Scroll the mouse wheel to zoom toward the cursor. Use + / − to zoom in steps, or ⊙ to reset the view.
Erase your work
Click the trash icon to delete all of your own drawings. Other users' drawings are not affected.
Collaborating
Anyone with the link shares the same canvas in real time. Active users are shown in the top bar — the coloured ring pulses while they're drawing or moving.
